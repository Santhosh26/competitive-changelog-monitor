🧪 Manual Testing Guide

Step 1: Open browser and navigate to http://localhost:8000

Step 2: Click through each tab (Feed → Digests → Health → Search → Trends)

Step 3: Test filters in Feed (competitor, tag, relevance, reviewed)

Step 4: Click "Mark Reviewed" button on an entry

Step 5: Click "Add Note" and verify modal opens

Step 6: Click "Share" and verify text is copied to clipboard

Step 7: Click on a digest title to expand and view content

Step 8: Search for a keyword (e.g., "security") and verify results

Step 9: Check Trends tab and verify charts render correctly

Step 10: Open browser DevTools → Console, verify no CSP violations
